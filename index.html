NEVER SHARE YOUR SEED PHRASE!

Your seed phrase is the master key to your cryptocurrency wallet.

If you were about to enter your seed phrase here, please stop immediately. This website is an educational tool designed to protect you.

Important Security Facts:

  • Never share your seed phrase with anyone - No legitimate service will ever ask for it
  • Never enter it online - Websites claiming to "validate" or "verify" seeds are scams
  • Store it securely offline - Write it down and keep it in a safe place
  • Beware of phishing - Scammers create fake validation tools to steal your funds

If you entered your seed phrase:

  • Your input was never saved or transmitted - it was cleared immediately
  • Move your funds to a new wallet with a fresh seed phrase
  • Consider your original seed phrase compromised if you've shared it anywhere

Remember: Legitimate wallet software never asks you to input your seed phrase online. Your seed phrase should only be used to restore your wallet in the official app on your own device.
